Hello! I am a three-wheeled Reliant Robin! I always like to think that I'm Ferrari red because I am fast as the speed of light, but my boss tells me that it's because 'Robin' is the my car model.. He's doing some small grocery shopping on Columbia Road and when he comes back I hope he will spread them out over the car, otherwise we might tip over...
